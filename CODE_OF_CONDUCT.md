# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respect for differing viewpoints, constructive feedback, responsibility, and a focus on the technical work. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, sexualized language, personal attacks, publishing private information, and conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's [security advisory form](https://github.com/sw1pp3r/kaspi-skill/security/advisories/new) with the subject `Code of Conduct`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential to the extent possible.
